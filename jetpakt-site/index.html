How it works

A Scan, then a Pulse.

01

Scan

One-time audit of your public reviews across Google and Yelp. Eight complaint pillars scored. Every finding is anchored to a verbatim public quote — nothing invented, no staff named.

02

Pulse

Recurring digest that watches your rating, review volume, complaint mix, and legal exposure. Alerts when rating drops 0.30★+, a new food-safety or wage flag appears, or the pillar mix shifts.

03

Drafts, not auto-sends

Every response template lands in your inbox as a draft. You review, edit, and send. Legal-HIGH items route to the owner only. Nothing goes public without your approval.

How we stay safe

Built to protect you, not embarrass you.

Defamation-safe quoting

Findings reference only verbatim public quotes already visible on Google or Yelp. We never name individual staff. We never invent a claim. We never repeat an unverified illness allegation as fact.

Legal-review flags

Wage, service-fee, billing, and food-safety language is flagged for your attorney before any response template is sent. HB25-1090 (effective Jan 1, 2026) is tracked automatically for Colorado operators.

Drafts-only delivery

Every response lands as a draft in your inbox. No auto-posting. No auto-email. You are the last decision every time.

Auditable workflow

Every Pulse run is snapshotted, diffed, and logged. You can reconstruct what we knew, when we knew it, and which template was suggested — down to the verbatim quote.
