Privacy and scope

What we do, what we do not do, and how your data is handled.

This page is written for operators, not lawyers. It covers what JetPakt Solutions stores, how long we keep it, what we never do with it, and the limits of our work. Plain language, no legalese, no buried surprises.

What we store

When a business works with JetPakt Solutions, we store the following, and only the following:

  • The business name, website, and public listing URLs the owner provides.
  • The owner's name, role, email, and optional phone number.
  • Public review text, ratings, and metadata drawn from Google, Yelp, and other publicly accessible review sources.
  • Public-record context relevant to the engagement (for example, statutes referenced for a legal-flag review).
  • The PDFs, drafts, and memos we deliver to the owner.
  • Basic operational logs (submission timestamps, delivery timestamps, email subject lines) for audit purposes.

We do not store credit card numbers. Payment is handled by Stripe; we only see a transaction reference. We do not store customer data from the owner's own systems (POS, CRM, reservations, staff records). We only read what is already public about the business.

How long we keep it

  • Active engagement data (reviews, PDFs, drafts) is kept for the life of the engagement and for 24 months after the final delivery, so the owner can reference prior work.
  • Inquiry form submissions that do not convert are kept for 12 months, then deleted.
  • Operational logs are kept for 24 months for audit, then deleted.
  • An owner can request earlier deletion at any time by emailing gojetpakt.us@outlook.com. We confirm deletion within seven business days.

What we never do

  • No posting. We never post, comment, reply, or publish on your behalf. Every response is a draft delivered to the owner's inbox. The owner reviews, edits if needed, and publishes.
  • No automation of judgment. Software finds the pattern. The owner makes the decision. We do not auto-request reviews, auto-reply to critics, auto-escalate, or auto-send anything that represents the business to the public.
  • No staff-naming. Our deliverables never name individual staff, even when reviewers do. Staff first names appear only inside a verbatim quoted review where we need to preserve the source, and in those cases we flag it for the owner's attention.
  • No data resale. We never sell, rent, or share owner data, inquiry data, or review data with any third party. The only places your data goes are: our own systems, the email address you provided, and Stripe (for billing, if you become a paying client).
  • No black-box scoring. Every pillar score, every severity rating, and every legal-flag trigger is shown with the verbatim quote that caused it. Owners can reconstruct our reasoning line by line.
  • No surveillance. We do not track your website visitors, set marketing cookies, or run analytics that identify individuals. The website uses only first-party, strictly necessary technical headers.

Scope of service

JetPakt Solutions works with independent, owner-operated businesses with fewer than five locations, across the United States. Typical industries include restaurants, cafés, salons, retailers, and service businesses. We do not work with national chains, franchise units, multi-state groups seeking automated posting, or marketing agencies reselling a reputation dashboard.

Our method is portable because the inputs are universal: public review text, public records, and the owner's own context. Jurisdiction-specific statutes are tracked when they apply to your business. For example, Colorado's HB25 1090 (Junk Fees Law) is tracked for Colorado operators. Comparable fee-disclosure and wage-disclosure statutes in other states are handled the same way.

Legal-flag disclaimer

JetPakt Solutions is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice. Legal-review flags in our deliverables are informational, not legal conclusions. We surface language from public reviews that may attract regulatory attention or private-action risk, and we route it to the owner for review by the owner's attorney. The owner's attorney makes the legal call. If you do not have an attorney, say so and we will flag that as an open item; we will not proceed with response drafts on flagged issues until the owner confirms attorney review is in place.

How to reach us

For any privacy, data-handling, or scope question, email gojetpakt.us@outlook.com. We reply personally, usually within one business day.
